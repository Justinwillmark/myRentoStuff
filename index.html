Frequently Asked Questions

Renters can access much-needed inventory financing based on their prepaid rent, without traditional collateral or extensive credit history. This helps them grow their business and increase sales.

You get a 90-day standard repayment period at 10% upfront interest. If there's an outstanding balance, it will be deducted from the next year's rent paid through the platform.

Landlords receive up to 7% bonus upfront on the rent paid by tenants through the platform, making it a more attractive and efficient way to manage rentals with no added risk.

Our platform uses bank-level encryption and security measures to protect all transactions and personal information. All payments are processed through secure payment gateways.
